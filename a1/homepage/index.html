Victor Lecomte - My Biography

This is my personal website

Overview

Born on the 11th of February of 1996. Currently studying Computer Science at University of Crete. Web designer, Photographer, Video Maker.

Web Design

I created my first website back in 2011, in the age of 13. One day I got an idea to create a website that would contain everything I loved: music and games. At first the music page was filled with YouTube music videos and the gaming page was full of mini-games found on major online gaming websites.

Roughly two years later, the next version of the website came up on my mind. The YouTube videos turned to an online music player and the mini-games were written off. Gaming videos and guides took their place.

Viclec.com is just a memory now. After puberty ended, I decided that there's no reason at all maintaining a website like this, without purpose. Until this day I have designed several websites along with the website of our family company (which happens to be my favorite one): www.multi.gr

Photography

Like many people I liked photography since I was a child, but I took the extra step of making it one of my main activities on 2013, when I bought my first reflex camera. I shortly realised that I loved spotting airplanes and I got into a club of planespotters in Heraklion. Apart from planes I love photographing animals, landscapes and generally nature -like a normal person.

Video Production

I'm the definition of an amateur, when it comes to video making. I started making gaming videos on YouTube and after that (and until this single day) I started making VLOGs about my travels. In the future I would like to start making short films.

The Future

I'm currently on my 3rd year in CSD. If everything goes as planned, I will graduate in four semesters. I would like to do a Master afterwards, in case I find a suitable matter to continue on. In other case I will work for our family buisiness, or I could even start my own company.
